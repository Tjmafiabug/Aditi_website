Training & Milestones

The making of an
artist.

From the first steps in Kathak in 2011 to a Guinness World Record in 2024 — every chapter of Aditi's journey has been shaped by discipline, devotion, and extraordinary teachers.

auto_stories
2011 — The Beginning

Foundation in Kathak

Began a five-year foundational course in Kathak under the guidance of Guru Rani Khanam. This formative period laid the technical and expressive bedrock of Aditi's artistry. Passed with First Division.

account_balance
2015 — National Institute

Kathak Kendra, New Delhi

Enrolled in the prestigious six-year Diploma Course at Kathak Kendra — the National Institute of Kathak, Ministry of Culture, Government of India. Completed in 2021 with First Division.

trending_up
2017 — Advanced Diploma

Guru Subhash Bhattacharya

Pursued a three-year Diploma in Kathak under the mentorship of Guru Subhash Bhattacharya, deepening her technical fluency and stage presence. Passed with First Division.

theater_comedy
Theatre

National School of Drama

Completed a one-year theatre certification from the National School of Drama, Delhi — adding a powerful dimension of abhinaya and dramatic expression to her dance vocabulary.

star
2020 — Honours Diploma

Guru Malti Shyam

Undertook a three-year Diploma with Honours under Guru Malti Shyam — the most rigorous chapter of formal training. Completed in 2023 with First Division. This period saw her emerge as a leading solo performer on national stages.

tv
Recognition

Doordarshan Graded Artist

Recognised as a Doordarshan Graded Artist — India's national broadcaster's mark of distinction for performing artists. A formal acknowledgment of excellence in classical arts.

emoji_events
2024 — World Record

Guinness World Record — Khajuraho

At Khajuraho — the ancient temple complex synonymous with India's classical arts heritage — Aditi participated in creating a Guinness World Record, marking a defining moment in her career and in Indian classical dance.

Ongoing

Prayag Sangeet Samiti

Currently pursuing specialisations in classical vocals and tabla from Prayag Sangeet Samiti — continuing to deepen the musical foundation that informs every aspect of her artistry.
