DOORDARSHAN GRADED ARTIST

A decade of devotion to the art of Kathak.

Aditi Vasistha is an accomplished Kathak artist from Delhi, shaped by the guidance of eminent gurus Rani Khanam, Malti Shyam, and Subhash Bhattacharya. Her career has seen her grace stages like Kamani Auditorium, Siri Fort, India Habitat Centre, and the President's House, New Delhi.

Beyond dance, Aditi brings deep musical breadth — tabla, harmonium, and bongo — and holds a theatre certification from the National School of Drama, Delhi, making her a truly multidisciplinary artist.
