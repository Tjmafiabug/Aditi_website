About Aditi

A story told through
movement and devotion.

Aditi Vasistha is an accomplished Kathak artist from Delhi — a Doordarshan graded performer whose decade-long journey spans some of India's most prestigious stages.

The Artist

Aditi's journey with Kathak began in 2011 under the watchful guidance of Guru Rani Khanam, and has since been enriched through training with Guru Subhash Bhattacharya and Guru Malti Shyam. She holds multiple diplomas from Prayag Sangeet Samiti and a six-year diploma from Kathak Kendra — the National Institute of Kathak — all passed with First Division.

Her decade-long career has seen her grace prestigious stages across the country: Kamani Auditorium, Siri Fort, India Habitat Centre, Triveni Kala Sangam, India International Centre, Dr BR Ambedkar International Centre, and the President's House, New Delhi.

The Performer

As a choreographer and performer, Aditi has created over 40 productions spanning solo, duet, and group formats — each one a testament to her versatile talent and artistic depth. She has captivated audiences across cities including Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Agra, Banaras, Ayodhya, Bangalore, Udaipur, Chandigarh, and Madhya Pradesh.

In 2024, she created a Guinness World Record at Khajuraho — a landmark moment in a career already marked by extraordinary achievement.

Beyond Dance

Aditi's artistry is not confined to dance alone. She commands the tabla, harmonium, and bongo — bringing a musician's ear to every choreographic decision. Her one-year certification from the National School of Drama, Delhi, adds a theatrical dimension that elevates her abhinaya beyond the conventional.

Currently, she is pursuing specialisations in classical vocals and tabla from Prayag Sangeet Samiti — deepening a musical foundation that already sets her apart.
